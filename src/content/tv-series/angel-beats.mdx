# Angel Beats!

An emotional roller coaster that blends high-octane action with lighthearted comedy and deep philosophical questions about life, regret, and the afterlife. Produced by P.A. Works and written by Jun Maeda, the series is renowned for its beautiful animation, memorable soundtrack, and a tear-jerking finale that leaves a lasting impact.

The story follows Otonashi, who awakens with no memories in an afterlife school populated by students who refused to pass on due to their regrets. He joins the Afterlife Battlefront in their fight against "Angel," the student council president, only to discover a much deeper truth about the world he now inhabits.
